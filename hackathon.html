What is a hackathon?

Mass collaboration is the engagement of a broad group of diverse participants in collective action toward a specific outcome. A mass collaboration event (also known as a hackathon or codeathon) is a weekend sprint development event where developers, technologists, entrepreneurs and other innovators work together to address specific challenges.
